# 🧭 The Story Behind UrbanMapper Community

UrbanMapper began as a research-driven project at **New York University**, with early design and development supported
by the university and the **OSCUR** program. We are grateful for that foundation and the research environment that made
UrbanMapper possible.

UrbanMapper Community continues that work as a community-led fork. Our goal is to keep the project welcoming,
maintainable, and transparent—while staying aligned with upstream innovations.

---

## Why a Community Fork?

We are committed to being kind and truthful in how we describe this transition. The motivation comes from practical
experience:

- **Response times sometimes took longer than expected.** On occasion, community questions waited up to four months.
  We understand that busy seasons happen and appreciate the effort that goes into maintaining a research project.
  Still, a community fork helps reduce waiting and encourages more distributed support.

- **The roadmap was not public or consistently visible.** For an open-source community, clarity about direction matters.
  UrbanMapper Community commits to a **public roadmap** that anyone can follow and contribute to.

- **We want to stay aligned with upstream while extending it.** We will keep an upstream sync and merge changes that
  align with the community fork, while building additional features in the open.

---

## Moving Forward Together

We welcome collaborators, contributors, and feedback. If you want to shape the future of UrbanMapper Community, please
visit the [Community Hub](community-hub.md) or open a discussion on GitHub.
